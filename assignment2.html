Swaminarayan

Teachings: Bhagwan Swaminarayan taught his devotees to live a life based on morality, faith in God, and service to others. Qualities: Bhagwan Swaminarayan was considered to have many divine qualities, including honesty, fearlessness, and trustworthiness. View on God: Swaminarayan's view on God was theanthropic, which is the idea that God's divine form is in a human shape. Incarnation: Bhagwan Swaminarayan incarnated on earth to establish ekantik dharma, save Dharma-Dev and Bhakti-Mata from demons, and take mumukshu bhaktas to Akshardham.

Dwarkadhis

The Dwarkadhish temple, also known as the Jagat Mandir and occasionally spelled Dwarakadheesh, is a Hindu temple dedicated to Krishna, who is worshiped here by the name Dwarkadhish (Dvārakādhīśa), or 'King of Dwarka'. The temple is located at Dwarka city of Gujarat, India, which is one of the destinations of Char Dham, a Hindu pilgrimage circuit.
